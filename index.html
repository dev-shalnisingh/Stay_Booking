Why I Created StayBooking

As a student myself, I faced a lot of difficulty finding reliable and affordable PGs and hostels when shifting to a new city. The available platforms were either confusing, outdated, or lacked trust.

That’s why I built StayBooking — a user-friendly, verified, and secure platform where students and professionals can easily discover suitable places to stay. It’s more than just a website — it’s my small effort to solve a common problem faced by many.
